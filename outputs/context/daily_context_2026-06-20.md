## Phenotypic Characterization of Unclassified Pulmonary Hypertension.
**链接**: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42318624/

**摘要**: Some symptomatic patients manifest pulmonary hypertension (PH), despite normal pulmonary vascular resistance and pulmonary artery wedge pressure-a condition termed unclassified PH. Although hypothesized to reflect increased flow as seen in congenital heart disease, broader clinical implications remain unknown.
